About

Have you ever wished instant messaging was a bit less, well, instant? Does seeing the "Seen" subtext below message make your heart rate jump up? Do you feel your anxiety levels rise when the "User is typing" message pops up?

You've come to the right place!
In fact, we even think e-mails are too quick of a medium.

The Lazy Post is a service that allows you to send a virtual postcard that will take at least as much time to arrive as a real card in the post. And we don't mean air mail, we mean boat.

So take your time, enjoy your vacation or wherever you're sending your postcard from. It will arrive, at some point..

A free service, by Théo and Jonathan.
